Meet Our Founder

Stella Uwaechue ACA • MSc • ACIPM • ANIMN • ANIPR • FIMC is an investor‑relations professional and financial‑literacy advocate with hands‑on experience across banking, investment banking and tech start‑ups. She holds a Distinction MSc (Media & Communication, Pan‑Atlantic University), a First‑Class BSc (Public Administration, OAU), and a Distinction OND (Business Administration, YABATECH).

An international event host and moderator, Stella inspires audiences through Doyenne Hangout masterclasses, The Stellar Life Show on YouTube and the Stella’s Musings podcast—delivering bold thought leadership on finance, career growth and personal transformation.
